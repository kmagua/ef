About IGFRD

Empowering Fiscal Transparency and Collaboration

At the heart of intergovernmental fiscal relations, the IGFRD County Portal serves as a central hub for sharing knowledge, fostering transparency, and enhancing collaboration between counties and the national treasury.

Our platform is designed to provide easy access to fiscal data, policy documents, and insightful resources, enabling informed decision-making and public engagement.
